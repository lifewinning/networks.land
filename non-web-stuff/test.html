Whenever talking about networks and information transfer things move from one place to another. moving them is hard and has a variety of problems.

Physical problems: How do you get something to travel a great distance?

How do you make sure the thing that was transmitted is the same thing that was eventually received ?

How do you ensure the message you're sending reaches the right receiver.

How do you allow many transmitters and receivers to play nicely with each other while still being efficient about your physical infrastructure.

How do you add redundancy so that if one node goes down the network can still operate.
